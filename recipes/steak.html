The perfect steak!

Description

Look at this steak! its got the crust and the herbs and the seasoning, its the best! Nothing beats a good steak!

You can have vegetables, baked potatoes, or salad with this beautiful steak! For dessert, why not try a nice slice of decadent cheesecake!

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • rosemary
  • salt
  • pepper
  • garlic powder
  • butter
  • a high temperature oil such as canola or avacado oil

Directions

  1. Unwrap your steak and use a paper towel to dry off the liquid.
  2. Season with salt and leave for at least one hour. You can use this time to prep your side item or dessert!
  3. Preheat a cast iron or stainless steel pan until it just starts to smoke.
  4. Pat your steak dry once more with a paper towel.
  5. Add a couple of tablespoons of high temp oil into your pan, then the steak.
  6. Cook each side for 4 minutes.
  7. add a generous amount of butter to the pan, and place the rosemary on the steak.
  8. use a spoon to baste the hot butter over the herbs onto the steak.
  9. When you are satisfied with the steak, remove and leave to rest. season with black pepper and garlic powder at this point.
  10. The steak will weep for a few minutes, mix with the spice, and when the temperature comes down, it will bring the spice liquid back into the steak!
  11. After 15 minutes of rest, you may slice the steak how you would like and enjoy!
