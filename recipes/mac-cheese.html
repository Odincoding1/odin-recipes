Mac and Cheese!

Description

Be amazed by this amazing mac and cheese with curly noodles! Can you feel it stick to your ribs? Can you feel your heart palpitate just looking at it?

This recipe is great alone, but also perfect for family gatherings! Your family will request it year after year!

Ingredients

  • Pasta of your choice, preferrably something thicker or with more substance (like elbows, fusili, bowties, etc.)
  • Sharp cheddar cheese, freshly grated.
  • Flour
  • Bacon (Try a thick cut bacon for some substance on top, or a thin cut for a crispy topping!)
  • half and half
  • Garlic Powder
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

Directions

  1. Take a large pot and boil water. When it is boiling, follow directions of pasta to make pasta al dente.
  2. On a cutting board, cut bacon into bite size peices.
  3. In a separate pan, add bacon and two tbsp of water. Turn heat to medium level. This will draw out the fat and help the bacon get crisp.
  4. When the bacon is to your liking, remove it and also remove the bacon fat to a separate container.
  5. Return 3 3tbsp of bacon fat to the pan, and add 3tbsp of flour to the fat. Make sure the burner is still on medium
  6. Continuously stir the flour for 3 minutes to cook out the raw flour taste.
  7. Add half and half a little at a time, and make sure each splash of liquid absorbs into the flour.
  8. When the mixture finally gets to a thick liquid state, you may add the rest of the half and half.
  9. Turn the heat off in the pan, and add your sharp cheddar cheese. stir until smooth.
  10. Add al-dente noodles to the sauce, and stir to combine.
  11. Scoop a generous portion of the mac and cheese into bowl and top with the reserved bacon bits. Enjoy!
